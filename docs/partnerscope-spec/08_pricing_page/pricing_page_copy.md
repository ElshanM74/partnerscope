## FAQ (accordion)

**Q: How is this different from our existing GRC tool?**  
A: We plug in as a data source, not a replacement. Findings export as JSON, CSV, Parquet; direct webhook to ServiceNow, Archer, OneTrust.

**Q: Do vendors have to pay?**  
A: No. The buyer pays; vendors respond to a questionnaire at no cost.

**Q: Who runs the red-team?**  
A: Our analysts, using a maintained catalogue aligned to OWASP LLM Top 10 (2025), MITRE ATLAS and NIST AI RMF. Sandbox only; production is never exposed.

**Q: GDPR / EU hosting?**  
A: Yes. All data stored in EU (Frankfurt). DPAs signed with every customer. Sub-processors listed on trust.partnerscope.eu.

**Q: Refunds?**  
A: Starter refundable within 24h if automated tests cannot complete. Pro and Enterprise refundable pro-rata for undelivered work.

**Q: Can we self-serve?**  
A: Starter and Pro yes. Enterprise requires a call for scoping (portfolio sizing, SSO, integrations).
